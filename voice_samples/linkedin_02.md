Got a chance to present my app VibeWatch to the Replit team at the AI Builders Meetup hosted by Replit x Razorpay yesterday.

VibeWatch is a mood-based movie discovery app I built on Replit. It asks you a few guided questions about your vibe and curates recommendations based on how you're feeling.

It was incredibly fun to demo something I built on Replit to the team itself, and even more exciting to get real-time feedback from Jeff Burke and Pranav Ganesh on the product and build experience.

I also had some great conversations with Anand Lakshmanan and the Razorpay team around how they are adopting AI internally.
Walking away with lots of ideas and learnings — excited to start implementing them into what I build next.

Sharing a few moments from the meetup. Always energizing to be around people building the future with AI.
