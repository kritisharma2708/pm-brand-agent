# Type: Product review / personal reflection

What if you could explore the world...without ever pulling out your phone?

Well recently I made a family trip to Greece along with our new companion, the RayBan Meta Glasses. And honestly, it delivered on all the hype and then some more.

From taking pictures from my point of view to answering any question I had about the historical monuments, all I had to say was "Hey Meta! What am I looking at?" "Hey Meta! Tell me more about this place" - and it would tell me everything about what was in front of me.

The best part was that I didn't have to take out my phone. No interruptions, no missed views. It captured everything just the way I was seeing it.

That's what I love about some products - they blend so seamlessly into your life that they don't feel like tech at all. It also made me realise how powerful products can be when hardware, AI, and design come together.

For once, I wasn't staring at a screen - I was just there. Maybe that's the future we actually need.
