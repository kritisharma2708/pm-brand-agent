# Platform: LinkedIn
# Type: Case study / PM thinking post

Netflix was made for bingeing. TikTok was built for swiping.
Ever wondered what would happen if they had a baby?

Recently, I got a chance to work on a fun and challenging problem statement "How will you grow Netflix if your own money was on the line?" At first, the question felt daunting but I began with identifying the core business of Netflix. I researched to find out the core challenges faced by Netflix today and its competitive landscape.

One key insight stood out: Gen Z spends hours scrolling through short-form content but when it comes to long-form platforms like Netflix, content fatigue is real.

So I asked: What if Netflix had a "Live & Trending" tab, a Reels-like feed of content snippets to help users discover what to watch next?
The goal:
- Improve content discovery
- Increase engagement + retention
- Unlock new monetization through ad snippets

I broke this down into:
- Business case
- Epics & user stories
- Wireframes
- Growth metrics
- Acceptance criteria for each flow

Huge shoutout to Karan Sethi for giving me this question and pushing me to think more deeply on how to approach a problem while aligning it with business.

Would love to hear your thoughts. What idea would you build for a platform like Netflix?
